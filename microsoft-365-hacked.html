What to Do If Your Microsoft 365 Account is Hacked

Follow these steps to recover your account and prevent future breaches.

Step 1: Immediately change your Microsoft 365 password and enable multi-factor authentication (MFA).

Step 2: Review recent account activity and remove any suspicious login sessions.

Step 3: Remove unknown email forwarding rules or permissions.

Step 4: Scan your devices for malware or viruses.

Step 5: Educate your team on phishing awareness to prevent future attacks.
