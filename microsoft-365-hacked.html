What to Do if Your Microsoft 365 Account Is Hacked

Step-by-step guide to recover and secure your Microsoft 365 account for your business.

When your Microsoft 365 account is compromised, it’s important to act fast. Here are the steps to recover your account and secure it against further attacks:

  1. Immediately change your password and ensure it’s strong.
  2. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) if not already set.
  3. Check account settings and forwarding rules for unauthorized changes.
  4. Scan devices for malware and remove threats.
  5. Contact Microsoft support for further guidance if needed.

Regular backups and monitoring help prevent future incidents.
