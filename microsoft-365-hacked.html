What to Do If Your Microsoft 365 Account is Hacked

Follow these steps to recover your account and prevent future breaches.

1. Immediately change your password and enable multi-factor authentication (MFA).

2. Check account activity and remove suspicious logins.

3. Alert your IT support provider for deeper investigation.

4. Review email forwarding rules and permissions.

5. Educate users on phishing awareness and security best practices.
