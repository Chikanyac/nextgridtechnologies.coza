What is MFA and Why It Matters

Learn how multi-factor authentication protects your business from account breaches.

MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) adds an extra security layer to login by requiring a code or authentication from a second device.

Benefits include:

  • Prevents unauthorized access even if passwords are compromised.
  • Reduces risk of phishing attacks.
  • Helps meet compliance requirements for sensitive business data.

Enable MFA for Microsoft 365 to secure emails, Teams, and other cloud services.
