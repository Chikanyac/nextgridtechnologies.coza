Top Cybersecurity Risks for Small Businesses

Understand the biggest threats facing small businesses in Gauteng and how to reduce risk.

Small businesses are often targets of cyberattacks due to limited IT resources. Key risks include:

  1. Phishing attacks targeting staff emails.
  2. Ransomware attacks on business data.
  3. Unpatched software vulnerabilities.
  4. Weak password and authentication practices.
  5. Insider threats or accidental data leaks.

Proactive monitoring, MFA, and regular staff training help mitigate these risks.
