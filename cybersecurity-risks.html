Top Cybersecurity Risks for Small Businesses

Learn the biggest threats and how to protect your company from cyber attacks.

• Phishing Attacks: Emails targeting employees to steal credentials.

• Ransomware: Malware that locks files and demands payment.

• Weak Passwords: Easily guessable passwords are a common vulnerability.

• Outdated Software: Unpatched systems are easy targets for hackers.

• Insider Threats: Employees or contractors misusing access.
