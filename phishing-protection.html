How to Stop Phishing Emails in Microsoft 365

Protect your business from phishing and spam with these practical steps.

1. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts.

2. Configure Microsoft 365 anti-phishing policies in the Security & Compliance Center.

3. Educate employees on identifying phishing emails.

4. Use advanced threat protection tools like Microsoft Defender for Office 365.

5. Regularly monitor logs for suspicious activity.
