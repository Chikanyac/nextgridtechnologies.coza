How to Stop Phishing Emails in Microsoft 365

Protect your business from phishing attacks with these Microsoft 365 security tips.

Phishing emails are a major threat to small businesses. To prevent them:

  1. Enable Microsoft Defender for Office 365.
  2. Set up anti-spam and anti-phishing policies.
  3. Train staff to identify suspicious emails.
  4. Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  5. Regularly review email logs for unusual activity.
